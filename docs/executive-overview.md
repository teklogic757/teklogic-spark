# Teklogic Spark AI Executive Overview

## What Spark AI Is

Teklogic Spark AI is a web-based platform that helps organizations turn workshop enthusiasm into a practical pipeline of AI and automation ideas. Instead of letting workshop notes fade away, Spark AI gives employees a simple place to submit ideas, get immediate AI-assisted feedback, and build visible momentum inside their company.

At a business level, the product solves a common post-workshop problem: people leave inspired, but there is no structured system to capture, prioritize, and sustain the best ideas. Spark AI turns that into a repeatable program with submissions, scoring, leaderboards, prompts, and lightweight administration.

## Who It Is For

Spark AI is designed for:

- consulting-led AI workshops that need a post-session system of record
- companies that want employee-generated AI use cases tied to their own operations
- leaders who want visibility into idea volume, idea quality, and engagement
- innovation teams that need a living backlog of practical automation opportunities

## Core Business Outcome

The software helps organizations move from abstract AI interest to a measurable internal adoption process.

That happens by combining four things:

- easy idea submission
- organization-aware AI evaluation
- gamified engagement through points and rankings
- admin visibility into users, organizations, workshop access, and content

## How The Experience Works

### 1. Users enter the platform

Employees sign in through a global login experience and are automatically routed to the correct organization workspace. Workshop guests can also join through access-code-driven workshop entry paths.

### 2. Ideas are submitted

Users describe a business problem, proposed solution, and optional supporting context. In desktop flows, they can also attach files to strengthen the submission.

### 3. AI evaluates the idea

Each idea is scored using organization-specific context such as industry, brand voice, business environment, and strategic details. The system produces:

- an overall score
- a reframed version of the idea
- feedback and coaching
- related suggestions

### 4. Momentum becomes visible

Ideas feed dashboards and leaderboards so users can see progress, compare participation, and stay engaged through contests or internal innovation challenges.

### 5. Admins manage the program

Super admins can manage organizations, users, invites, workshop access codes, training content, and supporting operational workflows from the admin area.

## Why It Is Different

Spark AI is not just a suggestion box and not just a chatbot wrapper.

Its differentiators are:

- it is built around company-specific AI idea capture, not generic AI experimentation
- it uses organizational context to improve relevance
- it creates visible engagement through ranking and contest mechanics
- it supports multi-tenant operation, so each organization has its own workspace
- it gives administrators enough control to operate the program, not just observe it
